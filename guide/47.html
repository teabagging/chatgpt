❓Two circular frames are kept one above the other. Frame X has a diameter of 16 cm and frame Y has a certain diameter. The fraction of the surface of frame X that is not covered by frame Y is 0.4375. What is the diameter of frame Y?

💡Let D_X be the diameter of frame X and D_Y be the diameter of frame Y.Given that the diameter of frame X is D_X = 16 cm.The radius of frame X is R_X = D_X/2 = 16/2 = 8 cm.The area of frame X is A_X = pi R_X^2 = pi (8)^2 = 64pi cm^2.Let D_Y be the diameter of frame Y.The radius of frame Y is R_Y = D_Y/2.The area of frame Y is A_Y = pi R_Y^2 = pi (D_Y/2)^2 = pi D_Y^2 / 4.The two circular frames are kept one above the other. We need to find the fraction of the surface of frame X that is not covered by frame Y.We assume that frame Y is placed above frame X, and they are concentric, or at least the center of frame Y is above the center of frame X when projected onto the plane of frame X.We are interested in the area of frame X that is covered by frame Y and the area of frame X that is not covered by frame Y.Since frame Y is kept above frame X, we consider the projection of frame Y onto the plane of frame X.We are given the fraction of the surface of frame X that is not covered by frame Y is 0.4375.Let A_{X cap Y} be the area of the intersection of frame X and frame Y when projected onto the plane of frame X. This is the area of frame X that is covered by frame Y.Let A_{X setminus Y} be the area of frame X that is not covered by frame Y.We are given that the fraction of the surface of frame X that is not covered by frame Y is 0.4375.So, frac{A_{X setminus Y}}{A_X} = 0.4375.We know that A_X = A_{X cap Y} + A_{X setminus Y}.Therefore, A_{X setminus Y} = A_X - A_{X cap Y}.So, frac{A_{X setminus Y}}{A_X} = frac{A_X - A_{X cap Y}}{A_X} = 1 - frac{A_{X cap Y}}{A_X} = 0.4375.From this, we can find the fraction of the surface of frame X that is covered by frame Y:frac{A_{X cap Y}}{A_X} = 1 - 0.4375 = 0.5625.A_{X cap Y} = 0.5625 times A_X = 0.5625 times 64pi.0.5625 = frac{5625}{10000} = frac{225}{400} = frac{9}{16}.So, A_{X cap Y} = frac{9}{16} times 64pi = 9 times 4pi = 36pi.The area of intersection A_{X cap Y} is the area of the region that is common to both frame X and frame Y.We are considering circular frames.
