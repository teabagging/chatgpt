❓After a meeting, Alice and Bob start jogging in opposite directions. Alice jogs 1 mile every 12 minutes and Bob jogs 3 miles every 40 minutes. How far apart are they after 2 hours?

💡Let A be the speed of Alice and B be the speed of Bob.Alice jogs 1 mile every 12 minutes.To find Alice's speed in miles per minute, we can calculate distance/time = 1 mile / 12 minutes.Alice's speed S_A = frac{1}{12} miles per minute.Bob jogs 3 miles every 40 minutes.To find Bob's speed in miles per minute, we can calculate distance/time = 3 miles / 40 minutes.Bob's speed S_B = frac{3}{40} miles per minute.They are jogging in opposite directions. So their relative speed is the sum of their individual speeds.Relative speed S_{rel} = S_A + S_B = frac{1}{12} + frac{3}{40} miles per minute.To add these fractions, we need a common denominator. The least common multiple of 12 and 40.Multiples of 12: 12, 24, 36, 48, 60, 72, 84, 96, 108, 120, ...Multiples of 40: 40, 80, 120, ...The least common multiple of 12 and 40 is 120.S_{rel} = frac{1}{12} times frac{10}{10} + frac{3}{40} times frac{3}{3} = frac{10}{120} + frac{9}{120} = frac{10+9}{120} = frac{19}{120} miles per minute.We need to find the distance apart after 2 hours.First, we convert 2 hours to minutes.1 hour = 60 minutes.2 hours = 2 * 60 minutes = 120 minutes.Distance = Speed × Time.Distance apart after 2 hours = Relative speed × Time in minutes.Distance apart = S_{rel} times 120 minutes.Distance apart = frac{19}{120} times 120 miles.Distance apart = 19 miles.We can also calculate the distance Alice and Bob jogged separately in 2 hours and then add them up.Time = 2 hours = 120 minutes.Distance Alice jogged = Alice's speed × Time = frac{1}{12} miles per minute × 120 minutes = frac{120}{12} miles = 10 miles.Distance Bob jogged = Bob's speed × Time = frac{3}{40} miles per minute × 120 minutes = frac{3 times 120}{40} miles = frac{360}{40} miles = frac{36}{4} miles = 9 miles.Since they are jogging in opposite directions, the total distance apart is the sum of the distances they each jogged.Total distance apart = Distance Alice jogged + Distance Bob jogged = 10 miles + 9 miles = 19 miles.Both methods give the same answer. Let's double check the calculations.Alice speed: 1 mile / 12 mins = 5 miles / 60 mins = 5 miles per hour.Bob speed: 3 miles / 40 mins = (3/40) * 60 miles per hour = 180/40 = 18/4 = 9/2 = 4.5 miles per hour.Relative speed = 5 + 4.5 = 9.5 miles per hour.Distance in 2 hours = Relative speed * time = 9.5 * 2 = 19 miles.This also gives the same result.
